Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our sign boards

Do you charge transportation and installation charges?

Yes, we charge transportation and installation fees, but the cost is very minimal and affordable. We ensure a smooth and professional installation process handled by experienced technicians to guarantee safety and perfect alignment.

Charges vary based on distance, board size, installation height, and required tools or equipment. All pricing details are shared transparently without hidden costs, ensuring the best value for your investment.

What is the price per sq. ft. for sign boards?

The price per sq. ft. differs based on the type of sign board you select, such as ACP, LED, Acrylic, Flex, Metal, or 3D Sign Boards. Each material and lighting option requires different manufacturing processes that influence cost.

We provide tailored quotations once we understand your measurement, design, and material preferences. For accurate pricing, feel free to share your requirement with TNSigns.

Do you provide sample projects to customers?

Yes, we provide sample project references and portfolio images to help customers understand our quality, finishing standards, and performance output. You can view our previous work completed for different industries.

We have delivered signage to shops, showrooms, offices, salons, hospitals, restaurants, and corporate brands. If needed, we can also arrange site visit references based on availability.

What types of sign boards do you manufacture?

We manufacture a wide range of indoor and outdoor signage, including Acrylic Sign Boards, LED Glow Sign Boards, ACP Boards, Metal & Stainless Steel Boards, Vinyl Printing Boards, and 3D Letter Sign Boards.

Our solutions are suitable for all business categories such as retail stores, restaurants, malls, hospitals, educational institutes, salons, showrooms, and corporate offices. Every product is built with premium materials for durability.

Do you offer custom sign board designs?

Yes, we provide fully customized sign board designs based on your brand identity, logo colors, and style preferences. You can customize size, thickness, font, finish, light effects, and materials.

Custom signage helps your business stand out and increases visibility. Whether you need custom LED boards, acrylic letters, ACP signage, or 3D logo signage, we guarantee professional finishing and precise craftsmanship.

Do you provide design mockups before production?

Yes, we offer digital mockups and preview designs before production begins. This allows you to clearly see the layout, color selections, lighting effects, and placement style before approval.

Mockups help avoid mistakes and ensure the final output matches your expectations and brand guidelines. Production starts only after your approval, ensuring confidence and clarity.

Can you deliver sign boards outside Chennai?

Yes, we deliver signage across Tamil Nadu and can arrange shipping to other locations based on requirement. We provide safe packing and logistics support to ensure secure delivery.

Delivery charges depend on distance, size, and transport type. For franchise chains or multi-location branding projects, we also offer coordinated dispatch and installation support.

Do you manufacture 3D letter sign boards?

Yes, we specialize in premium 3D letter sign boards including Acrylic 3D Letters, Stainless Steel Letters, Brass Letters, LED Backlit 3D Letters, and Front-lit 3D Signage. These provide a modern and elegant look.

3D signage is ideal for showrooms, offices, malls, hotels, spas, salons, and high-visibility storefronts. They are waterproof, weatherproof, and long-lasting for indoor and outdoor use.

What is the difference between 2D and 3D sign boards?

2D sign boards are flat surface boards without depth, commonly used for basic shop boards, indoor branding, directional signage, and economical display needs.

3D sign boards have raised letters that create depth, visibility, and a premium appearance. They use materials like acrylic, metal, or LED lighting and are ideal for businesses wanting a stylish and eye-catching sign.
