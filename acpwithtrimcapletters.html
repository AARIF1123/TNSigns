About ACP Sign Boards

An ACP sign board is one of the most popular choices for modern business branding. ACP stands for Aluminium Composite Panel, which is known for its strength, durability, and sleek finish. When combined with Trimcap letters, the board creates a stylish 3D look that makes any brand name stand out. Businesses prefer ACP sign boards because they are weather-resistant, lightweight, and provide a premium appearance at an affordable cost.

If you are looking to make your storefront or office more attractive, an ACP sign board with Trimcap letters is a perfect solution.

Features of ACP Sign Boards with Trimcap Letters:

  • Made with high-quality Aluminium Composite Panel for durability.
  • Trimcap letters give a 3D and colorful border effect.
  • Suitable for shops, showrooms, restaurants, offices, and malls.
  • Weather-resistant and long-lasting – perfect for outdoor branding.
  • Lightweight and easy to install compared to traditional boards.
  • Provides a modern, premium look that catches customer attention.

An ACP sign board is not just a display – it’s a branding investment. Whether you want to highlight your logo, store name, or promotional message, this type of board ensures visibility both day and night.
