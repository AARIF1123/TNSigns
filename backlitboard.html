Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about backlit sign boards

What is a backlit signboard?

A backlit signboard is an illuminated signage solution where LED lights are placed behind acrylic or metal letters to create a bright and attractive glowing effect. It helps businesses improve visibility both during the day and at night.

Why choose LED backlit sign boards for my business?

LED backlit sign boards offer high brightness, energy efficiency, and long life. They give your shop or office a premium appearance and attract more customers compared to regular non-illuminated boards.

Yes. Our outdoor LED backlit sign boards are waterproof, weather-resistant, and built to withstand Chennai's climate conditions.
